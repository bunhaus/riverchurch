Join us for River Church

Brethren, we bid you join us for our annual pilgrimage of the holy floatilla. Come baptize your souls in river water and white claw.

Genesis of the Float

1 In the beginning, God created the Taylor on the earth. 2 Now the Taylor was uncelebrated and thirsty, darkness was over the surface of the river, and the Spirit of God was hovering over the waters.

3 And God said, “Let there be lite beer” and there was lite beer. 4 God saw that the lite beer was good, and he separated the lite beer from the white claw. 5 God called the lite beers “dank,” and the white claws he called “nice.” And there was evening, and there was morning—the first day.

6 And God said, “Let there be a vault upon the waters to separate our butts from the river surface.” 7 So God made the vault and separated the water under the vault from the butts above it. And it was so. 8 God called the vault “floaties.” And there was evening, and there was morning—the second day.

20 And God said, “Let the floaties teem with partying creatures, and let their celebration fly above the earth across the sky.” 21 So God created the partying creatures, according to their kinds. And God saw that it was good. 22 God blessed them and said, “Be fruity and blast your boomboxes and do backflips off big logs , and let the baddies increase on the earth.” 23 And there was evening, and there was morning—the third day.

31 God saw all that he had made, and it was very good.

Event Details

Date: Saturday, July 25th 2025

Schedule:

  • 10:30 AM - Riverside brunch (beat the crowds to secure parking - and chill with us while the river warms up)
  • 12:00 PM - Flotilla launch! (please arrive at least 30mins prior to blow up tubes and stage for launch)
  • Float duration - Usually 3-5 hours
  • Late afternoon - Afterparty burgers & drinks at a nearby residence

Location:

  • Parking (and getting back to your car) - This is a one-way float down the Snoqualmie River. We recommend arranging a carpool with other attendees and leaving most cars at the end point, as parking at the start point is limited. Other options include Ubering to the start point, locking up a bike at the opposite end, or pre-purchasing a spot on Fall City Floating’s shuttle (drops off 0.2 miles from our start point).
  • Float Start Point: Plum #1 River Access Point, 37198 SE Fish Hatchery Rd Fall City WA 98024. This is a small parking lot with a trail down to the river. Parking requires a Discover Pass, and the spots fill up early. (If lot is full, there is sometimes roadside parking available half a mile further down 202.)
  • Float End Point: Fall City Bar & Grill, 33723 Redmond-Fall City Rd SE Fall City WA 98024. We’ll get out of the river (near) here. There is lots of free parking.
  • Afterparty: 29301 NE 11th St Carnation WA 98014. We’re always starving at the end of the float. Fortunately, Kate lives <10mins away and is grilling up burgers for everyone.

Recommended Packing List:

  • Tube
  • Water/drinks/snacks (no glass)
  • Sun protection (sunscreen, sunglasses, hat, shirt, etc)
  • Water shoes or sandals that strap on securely
  • Waterproof bag (for phone, keys)
  • Tube patch kit
  • Discover Pass (if parking at start point)
