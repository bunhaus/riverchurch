We recommend arranging a carpool with other attendees and leaving most cars at the end point, as parking at the start point is limited. Other options include Ubering to the start point, locking up a bike at the opposite end, or pre-purchasing a spot on Fall City Floating’s shuttle (drops off 0.2 miles from our start point). * Float Start Point: Plum #1 River Access Point, 37198 SE Fish Hatchery Rd Fall City WA 98024. This is a small parking lot with a trail down to the river. Parking requires a Discover Pass, and the spots fill up early. (If lot is full, there is sometimes roadside parking available half a mile further down 202.) * Float End Point: Fall City Bar & Grill, 33723 Redmond-Fall City Rd SE Fall City WA 98024.
